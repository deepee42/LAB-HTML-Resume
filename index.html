Dennis Patton - Work History

Freelance Musician/Songwriter/Producer

1993 to Present

Performed various tasks of producing, arranging & composing musical works on a contractual basis. Producing and managing budgets and overseeing all aspects of creating a musical project for various record companies including Provident, Reunion, Benson, Diadem, Word, Sparrow, BMG and others. Performed live with various touring artists including Bob Carlisle, Allies, Susan Ashton, The Imperials and others. Accolades include arranging Billboard #1 song and included performances on “The Tonight Show”, “Regis”, “Oprah”, “Good Morning America” and others. Nominated for a Dove Award for Producing “Children’s Music Album of the Year”.

Software Beta Tester - Spectrasonics

2000 to 2015

Responsible for testing software instruments created by Spectrasonics before they are released to the public.

Creative Director - Autumn Records

2004 - 2009

Responsible for managing, creating, authoring, and producing audio/video projects exclusively for Autumn Records. Designed and assembled a state-of-the-art digital/analog recording facility on Music Row (1616 16th Ave. South) currently McPherson Studios. Job duties consisted of discovering talent, developing talent, hiring talent, maintaining budgets, managing, maintaining, and upgrading studio facility.

Creative Director - Butterfly Music Group

2003 - 2004

Responsible for managing, creating, authoring, and producing audio/video projects exclusively for Butterfly Music Group. Collaborating, commuting & recording from facilities in Nashville and Las Vegas.

Staff Songwriter - Reunion Records

1995 - 1999

Authored and co-authored songs exclusively for Reunion Publishing. Accolades include co-authoring #1 nationally charted single. Co-authoring #1 SESAC song.
